Alankars

Alankars are very important to know because it will helps you to know the keys properly and will increase the fingers speed to catch it . There are 21 alankars to practice , to make you perfect .Alankars are fundamental exercises in Indian classical music, used to develop tonal accuracy, melodic agility, and control over the notes. Practicing them on the harmonium can enhance your understanding of the scale and help you develop better technique for your vocals. So, practice it properly and gain the knowlegde of harmonium.
